Non-model features that would add more value than new models:

Soil test report upload — farmer uploads a photo of their soil test report, OCR extracts the values, auto-fills the fertilizer tool. Removes the biggest friction point.
WhatsApp integration — Indian farmers use WhatsApp more than websites. A WhatsApp bot that answers "which crop should I grow?" would reach 10x more users than the website.
Offline mode (PWA) — many farmers have poor connectivity. Making the site installable and cacheable means it works in the field without internet.
Mandi prices widget — live crop prices from government AGMARKNET API on the News page. Directly useful every day.
Kisan Credit Card / PM-KISAN scheme info — government scheme eligibility checker. Farmers miss out on benefits they qualify for simply because they don't know about them.
YouTube farming channel recommendations — curated by crop and region, already partially done in the News page.
